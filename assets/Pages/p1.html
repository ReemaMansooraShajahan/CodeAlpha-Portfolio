IoT based immobility patients monitoring system

A Communication service

Our IoT-based immobility patient monitoring system addresses the challenges faced by individuals with paralysis, providing a cost-effective and user-friendly solution to enhance independence. The core of the system relies on an ESP32 microcontroller, overseeing hand motion recognition through an MPU-6050 (Accelerometer & Gyro) and temperature sensor. This enables patients to convey their needs via gestures, establishing a meaningful means of communication.

The system continuously monitors vital signs, such as heartbeat and temperature, triggering alerts if values deviate from normal ranges. The receiver system processes and displays this information on an LCD screen, ensuring immediate awareness for caretakers and medical professionals. Simultaneously, the data is transmitted online to an IoT server, allowing remote access to real-time health metrics. Integration of the ESP32 Wi-Fi module facilitates easy access to patient health conditions via smartphones, providing family members and doctors with valuable insights.

Emphasizing power efficiency, security, and scalability, our solution ensures reliability and sustainability. By combining gesture-based communication, vital sign monitoring, and remote accessibility, our project aims to significantly improve the quality of life for immobility patients. This empowers them to communicate effectively and receive timely medical attention in the comfort of their homes, bridging the gap between innovative healthcare technology and personalized patient care.
